Birth Doula Services

What is a doula?

As a birth doula, I will meet with you and your partner at least two times prior to birth, in order to discuss your birth preferences and coping techniques. When you go into labor, I will either meet you at your home or at your birth place, depending on your preference. I will stay with you throughout your entire birth, and will wait until you and your baby are comfortably recovering together before leaving.
